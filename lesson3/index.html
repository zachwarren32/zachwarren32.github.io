Site Purpose

The purpose of "Weather on the Horizon" is to help you plan out your adventures that take weather into consideration. Everyone enjoys being outside and enjoying nature and we completely agree. There is no better way to accompany a great adventure than with some great weather. Go enjoy the awesome wonders of the earth without the bummers of bad weather.
